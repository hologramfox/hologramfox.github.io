Emotional Art Generation

Welcome to the gallery of Emotional Art generated by GAN (neural network used to generate images)! In our project we aim to generate Emotional Art. There are 8 categories to describe emotional reaction to a picture distinguished in literature:

Positive

  • amusement
  • awe
  • contentment
  • excitement

Negative

  • anger
  • disgust
  • fear
  • sad

You are welcome to vote what emotion (positive or negative) the image evoked in you! There are no right answers. If you don't know what to choose - take the first impression you have in mind.
